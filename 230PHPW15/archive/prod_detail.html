Anti-Bite Gloves

These gloves are made from very strong cowhide leather and are very difficult for your spiders to bite through. Use them when cleaning the cage(s) of particularly aggressive spiders and tarantulas, or if you're just an individual who simply wants to avoid a bite at all costs.
